## 🔄 Complete Workflow

**Step 1: CAPTURE IDEAS** 💡
```
personal-ip/ideas/concepts/my-idea.md
- Problem statement
- Initial thoughts
- Research directions
```

**Step 2: PLAN & DEVELOP** 🔨
```
personal-ip/projects/active/2026-06-my-project/
- README.md (overview)
- SPEC.md (technical details)
- STATUS.md (weekly updates)
- CODE/ (implementation)
```

**Step 3: SHOWCASE & SHARE** 🏆
```
personal-ip/portfolio/case-studies/2026-06-my-project.md
- What was built
- Why it matters
- Results & impact
- Link to implementation
```
